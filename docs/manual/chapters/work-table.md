## Rule it out in 10

A work table is a **task table**: a sturdy, function-first surface built for kitchen, craft, or shop use, usually showing **honest work wear**, often with a drawer, bin, shelf, or stretcher for the job.

If the table is decorative and balanced for a parlor, it is a parlor or occasional table. The work table is defined by **utility first**: durability, task fittings, and the marks of real use.

## What it is

The work table is where things got done: food prepped, dough rolled, sewing and crafts worked. Function shapes it, so it is usually **more durable and plainer** than formal tables, with a thick or scrubbed top, simple sturdy legs, and task-specific fittings (a deep drawer, a bin, a lower shelf). Expect **stains, knife cuts, scrubbing wear, and old utility repairs**, which are honest character, not damage.

A refined cousin is worth knowing: the **sewing or work table** of the Federal and Victorian parlor, a small elegant table with fitted compartments and sometimes a cloth **work bag** hanging below. That is a decorative form, the opposite end from a scrubbed kitchen table, though both wear the "work table" name.

## On the short list, confirm and date it

- Confirm the **task-first character** and any work fittings.
- Read the **wear** as evidence of real use and age, then date by **construction**: joinery, fasteners, saw marks, and secondary woods (Method section).
- For a refined sewing table, date by drawer construction, inlay, and legs, like a Pembroke.

## Do not confuse it with

| Form | What it really is | The tell that separates it |
|---|---|---|
| **Work table** | Function-first task table | Sturdy, worn, task fittings |
| Parlor or occasional table | Decorative table | Balanced, ornamented, room-facing |
| Sewing table | Refined fitted work table | Small, fitted interior, sometimes a work bag |
| Hoosier cabinet | Kitchen workstation case | A tall case with flour bin, not a table |

## What hurts the value
- **Over-restoration** that sands away the honest work surface (on country pieces the scrubbed top is the character buyers want).
- **A married or replaced top**.

## What it is worth, in plain terms
A plain country **kitchen work table** is **common and modest**, though scrubbed-top farmhouse tables have a steady, friendly market. A fine **Federal sewing table** with inlay and an original bag is **desirable and premium**. Honest surface, original fittings, and construction set the range.

## Quick field routine
First, confirm task-first character and work fittings.
Second, read the wear as honest use.
Third, date by construction.
Fourth, separate the scrubbed kitchen work table from the refined sewing table.
