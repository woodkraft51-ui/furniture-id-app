# Master Illustration List

> Every image the guide needs, added as Claude identifies it, chapter by chapter and atlas by
> atlas. Owner provides hero photos (own pieces) and may swap anything. Claude builds the teaching
> diagrams (Mermaid trees, tables, SVG line art) and finds CC0 or public-domain photo links where
> they exist. "Format" says who makes it.
>
> Status key: NEEDED (to make), DRAFTED (Claude made a version), HAVE (owner supplied), SWAP (owner wants different).

## Format legend
- **Owner photo**: owner shoots from inventory (PhotoRoom, wide canvas, generous margin).
- **Claude diagram**: Mermaid decision tree or flowchart, or hand-built SVG line diagram.
- **Claude table**: rendered in the page, no art file needed.
- **CC0 link**: Claude finds a verified public-domain or CC0 image; owner downloads or swaps.
